RECIPE


    For the Pizza Dough
    • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) of active dry yeast
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • 3/4 cup warm water
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil

    For the Pizza Topping
    • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
    • 1 1/2 cups fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced or shredded
    • Fresh basil leaves
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
    • Extra-virgin olive oil


PROCEDURE


    Prepare the Pizza Dough
    • In a small bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.

    Mix the Dough
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil.
    • Stir everything together until a dough forms.

    Knead the Dough
    • Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead it for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. You can also use a stand mixer with a dough hook.

    Rise the Dough
    • Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a damp cloth or plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours or until it doubles in size.

    Preheat the Oven
    • Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can go (usually around 500°F or 260°C) and place a pizza stone or an inverted baking sheet in the oven while it preheats.

    Shape the Pizza
    • Punch down the dough and divide it into two portions. Roll out each portion on a floured surface into a round pizza crust.

    Assemble the Pizza
    • Place the pizza dough on parchment paper to make it easier to transfer to the oven.
    • Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
    • Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
    • Tear fresh basil leaves and scatter them over the cheese. Season with salt and pepper.

    Bake the Pizza
    • Using a pizza peel or an inverted baking sheet, carefully transfer the pizza (on parchment paper) onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven.
    • Bake for about 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

    Finish and Serve
    • Remove the pizza from the oven, drizzle with extra-virgin olive oil, and let it cool for a minute.
    • Slice and serve your homemade Margherita pizza hot!
