RECIPE


    -200g pork belly
    -10cm scallion
    -3 red chilis
    -2 laurels
    -8 cloves of garlic
    -1 cinnamon stick
    -1 tbsp sugar
    -1 star anise
    -1 tbsp soy sauce
    -1 tbsp dark soy sauce
    -1 tsp salt
    -1 tsp chicken stock powder
    -1 english muffin
    -1 shishito green pepper


PREPARATION


    - Cut the meat into 1 inch

    A: 4cm green onion
    - 4pcs of ginger
    - 1 tbsp cooking wine

    B: 6cm green onion
    - 2pcs laurel
    - 3pcs chili peppers
    - 4 pcs of ginger
    - 1 cinnamon stick
    - 1star anise

PROCEDURE


    - Fill the Stock Pan with water
    - put the pork in pot
    - add A and boil for about 30 minutes
    - after boiling, remove the pork
    - prepare another Pan
    - put cooking oil and sugar
    - When it starts to turn a little brown, add the meat again.
    - add B The trick is to add the ingredients in order
    - Add hot water and simmer for about 40 minutes.
    - Add salt and chicken stock and let it simmer for another 10 minutes
    - on the other Pan, To save time, with english muffin, To give the English muffin a crunchy feel, fry both side
    - after all of that, chopped the pork into small pieces and filled it to muffin
