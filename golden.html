RECIPE


    • 1 piece of red snow crab
    • 3 egg yolks
    • A small amount of bamboo shoots
    • A moderate amount of ginger (chopped)
    • 1tbsp shaoxing wine
    • Cultivate a small amount
    • Granulated sugar a small amount
    • Appropriate amount of soft flour
    • Mizuki potato starch (1tsp) Water (1tsp)


PREPARATION


    • Add salt and sugar to the egg yolk and let it sit for about 3 hours.
    • Finely chopped ginger and bamboo shoots
    • Take out all the meat and remove the claws and shell ball.
    • Prepare soft flour and dissolved potato starch and fry the crab parents and shells.

PROCEDURE


    • Heat the coil and add the ginger and bamboo shoots
    • Add crab meat
    • Place the egg yolk in a steamer and steam for 10 to 15 minutes
    • Add oil to the pot and add the egg yolks
    • Completed! It's delicious when you mix crab meat and egg yolk together.
