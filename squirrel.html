RECIPE


    • 1 green bell pepper
    • 1/2 carrot
    • 1/4 bamboo shoot
    • 3 shiitake mushrooms
    • 1.5kg threeline grunt
    • 1tbsp salt
    • 2tbsp shaoxing wine
    • 5g bite-size green onion
    • 2g minced ginger
    • 80g starch
    • 1tbsp cooking oil
    • 8g minced green onion
    • 5g minced garlic
    • 3g minced ginger
    • 4tbsp ketchup
    • 1tsp salt
    • 2tbsp sugar
    • 2tbsp shaoxing wine
    • 2tbsp vinegar
    • 2tbsp water
    • 1tbsp starch with water
    • 1 mini tomato
    • a little pine nut


PREPARATION


    • First, we'll cut the green peppers, carrots, shiitake mushrooms, and bamboo shoots.
    • Cut the green onion into 2cm wide pieces and chop the rest. Also chop the garlic and ginger.
    • Wash the fish with water, add salt, Shaoxing wine, green onions and ginger, and let it sit for about 10 minutes.
    • Applying potato starch evenly to the fish and sprinkling it on the cuts will help open the fish.

PROCEDURE


    • Pour plenty of oil into a pot, heat it up, and fry the fish. There's a lizard's tail
    • After frying the fish, Stir- fried garlic, green onion and ginger, ketchup, Shaoxing wine. Add salt, sugar, vinegar, and water- soluble potato starch
    • Once fragrant, add the vegetables and stir fry.
    • Pour the sauce you made on the fish and it's done!
