RECIPE


    For the Kebab
    • 500 grams of thinly sliced doner kebab meat (usually beef or lamb)

    For the Tomato Sauce
    • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
    • 1 cup of crushed tomatoes
    • 1 clove of garlic, minced
    • 1 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (adjust to your spice preference)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

    For the Yogurt Sauce
    • 1 cup of plain yogurt
    • 1 clove of garlic, minced
    • Salt to taste

    For Serving
    • 4 large pieces of pita bread or lavash
    • Butter for drizzling
    • Fresh parsley, chopped
    •Red pepper flakes (optional)


PROCEDURE


    Preparing the Tomato Sauce
    • In a saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
    • Stir in the tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally until it thickens.

    Making the Yogurt Sauce
    • In a bowl, mix the plain yogurt, minced garlic, and a pinch of salt. Stir until well combined.

    Assembling the Iskender Kebab
    • Warm the pita bread or lavash in the oven or on a stovetop griddle.
    • Place the warm bread pieces on serving plates.
    • Arrange the thinly sliced doner kebab meat over the bread.
    • Drizzle melted butter over the meat.
    • Pour the hot tomato sauce generously over the meat and bread.
    • Spoon the yogurt sauce on top of the tomato sauce.

    Finishing Touches
    • Sprinkle freshly chopped parsley over the Iskender Kebab for freshness and color.
    • Optionally, you can sprinkle some red pepper flakes for extra heat.
    • Serve the Iskender Kebab immediately while it's hot.
